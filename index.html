ABOUT

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Sciences at UW-Madison, advised by Prof. Kangwook Lee. Before that, I obtained Bachelor's degree in Data Science from Yuanpei College, Peking University. My research interest lies in machine learning, with a recent focus on sequential learning problems including reinforcement learning, diffusion models and LLMs.
